Japanese Otter

Otters have webbed feet!

  • Scientific Name: Lutra Nippon
  • Average Length: between 65 and 80 cm (25.5 and 31.5 in) long
  • Average Lifespan: ~25 years
  • Habitat: Rivers in Japan

The Japanese river otter is an extinct variety of otter formerly widespread in Japan. Dating back to the 1880s, it was even seen in Tokyo.
